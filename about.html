About me

A little
about me

I'm Kantisa, a student based in Malaysia with a strong interest in UI/UX design and frontend web development. I enjoy exploring how digital interfaces are built and how design decisions can improve user experience and usability. Through my learning journey, I have developed skills in creating clean, responsive, and user-friendly web interfaces using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Tailwind CSS. I am particularly interested in modern web design practices, layout structure, and building websites that are both functional and visually appealing. This personal blog serves as a space for me to document my learning progress, share academic projects, and improve my understanding of real-world web development workflows. I am continuously learning and improving my skills in both design and development.

I focus on creating user-friendly digital experiences that solve basic problems in a practical and effective way.
